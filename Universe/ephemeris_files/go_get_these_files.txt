EMTG does not redistribute large BSP files. Download the kernels required by
your mission from the official NASA NAIF archive.

Planetary BSP directory:
https://naif.jpl.nasa.gov/pub/naif/generic_kernels/spk/planets/

Direct link to the traditional EMTG planetary kernel:
https://naif.jpl.nasa.gov/pub/naif/generic_kernels/spk/planets/de430.bsp

All generic kernels:
https://naif.jpl.nasa.gov/pub/naif/generic_kernels/

Mission archives and body-specific kernels:
https://naif.jpl.nasa.gov/pub/naif/

Place downloaded files in this directory. A typical planetary setup includes:

de430.bsp
naif0012.tls
pck00010.tpc

Also get the body-specific SPICE kernels for any body that you need that is not part of de430.
